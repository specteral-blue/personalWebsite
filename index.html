About Me
I’m Bella, a 9th grader with a passion for web development. Even at my young age, I’ve delved into the world of coding and built a strong foundation in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. My interest in technology started with a fascination about how websites are made, and through a lot of learning and hands-on practice, I’ve managed to create some pretty cool projects. From simple personal sites to more complex web applications, I love experimenting and seeing my ideas come to life on the screen.

One of the things I enjoy most about web development is designing user-friendly and visually appealing interfaces. It’s not just about writing code for me; it’s also about solving problems and figuring out how to make everything work smoothly. Whether it’s for a school assignment or a personal project, I always strive to improve my skills and tackle new challenges with enthusiasm.

I’m also exploring various frameworks and libraries to expand my development toolkit. Tools like React or Vue.js are exciting to me because they help build dynamic and responsive web applications more efficiently. I’m eager to keep learning and adapting to new technologies, as I know this will help me stay current in the ever-evolving tech world.

Looking forward, I’m excited about the future of web development and what’s next for me. I plan to continue developing my skills and exploring different areas of the field, such as backend development or UX/UI design. With my passion and dedication, I’m optimistic about making a meaningful impact in the tech industry as I grow and advance in my career.
